# Auto Mechanics The Blessing of God

## INTRO

The purpose of this project is to provide the auto mechanic company "Auto Mechanics The Blessing of God" with a simple website.

## Description

The website will be a relatively simple, static, multi-page project. We will make use of html, css, js, GitHub, VS Code, and Cloudflare.

The language of the site will be Spanish. It will have a single page. At the top there will be a navbar with the logo and name of the business on the left. Then, on the right it will have the links to the sections Servicios and Contacto, and then a button called Haz Una Cita which will automatically take you to call the business phone number. After the navbar, we will have a hero with a business intro. After that, we will have the sections mentioned above. Then we will have a footer.

The color palette of the website will be black as background, green as color 1 and white as color 2. Other accents of these colors may be used.
